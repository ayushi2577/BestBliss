# Project Brief: BestBliss Luxury Hotel Experience

## 1. Brand Identity & Vision
**Brand Name:** BestBliss Hotels & Resorts
**Vision:** To provide a sanctuary of "Gilded Serenity" where modern technology meets timeless luxury. The experience is designed for the discerning traveler who values comfort, elegance, and seamless digital interaction.

## 2. Design System: Gilded Serenity
The visual language is defined by a sophisticated dark-mode aesthetic with high-contrast gold accents.
*   **Color Palette:** Deep Charcoals (#121414), Rich Golds (#D4AF37), and soft "Glow" accents for interactive elements.
*   **Typography:** Playfair Display for elegant, high-end headings and a clean, modern sans-serif for functional UI.
*   **Visual Style:** Glassmorphism, high-fidelity lifestyle photography, and generous whitespace to create a sense of breathability and exclusivity.

## 3. Core Guest Journey (Mobile & Desktop)
The platform covers the full guest lifecycle, from pre-arrival to in-room experience.

### A. Discovery & Booking
*   **Landing Page:** High-impact hero sections showcasing dining, stay, and lifestyle.
*   **Booking Engine:** A streamlined reservation flow with premium "Room Cards" highlighting exclusive suites like the Royal Penthouse and Azure Lagoon Villa.

### B. Access & Authentication
*   **Login Experience:** Split-screen (desktop) and centered (mobile) layouts using atmospheric brand imagery to welcome members back to their sanctuary.

### C. The Guest Dashboard (In-Stay)
*   **Digital Room Key:** High-visibility "Secure Access" feature for seamless room entry.
*   **Service Shortcuts:** Rapid access to In-Room Dining, Housekeeping, Valet, and Concierge services.
*   **Personalization:** Contextual greetings and "Tonight's Special" curated recommendations.

### D. Smart Room Control
*   **Climate & Lighting:** Precise control over room temperature and curated lighting "Scenes" (Relax, Read, Sleep, Morning).
*   **Environmental Monitoring:** Real-time feedback on air quality and humidity.
*   **Curtain Control:** Intuitive slider-based opacity and position management.

### E. Services & Dining
*   **In-Room Dining:** A digital menu featuring categorized gourmet selections (Starters, Main Course, Desserts) with integrated cart management.

### F. Stay Management
*   **My Stays:** A clear overview of upcoming, current, and past experiences with easy access to receipts and rebooking.

## 4. Technical Requirements
*   **Responsive Design:** Full parity between Mobile and Desktop experiences using a unified horizontal top navigation (Desktop) and iconic bottom navigation (Mobile).
*   **Accessibility:** High contrast ratios for golden text on dark backgrounds.
*   **Animation Intent:** Smooth transitions for glassmorphic cards and subtle pulse effects for active digital keys.
